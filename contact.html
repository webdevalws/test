Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for major events like weddings and corporate functions. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines depending on availability.

Do you offer customized packages?

+

Yes! We understand that every event is unique. We work with you to create a customized package that fits your specific needs and budget.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellations made 60 days before the event receive a full refund minus a processing fee. Cancellations within 30-60 days receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations within 30 days of the event.

Can I visit your office for consultation?

+

Absolutely! We encourage in-person consultations. Please schedule an appointment by calling us or filling out the contact form, and we'll arrange a convenient time for you.

Do you provide services outside the city?

+

Yes, we service events within a 100-mile radius. For events beyond this range, additional travel fees may apply. Contact us to discuss your specific location.
